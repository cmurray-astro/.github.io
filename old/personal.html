About Me

I grew up in Brooklyn, New York. Traveling around "the city" on foot is my favorite pasttime. I moved to Minnesota in 2007 to attend Carleton College, where I learned to notice and appreciate the stars at Goodsell Observatory. In the summer of 2010, I spent a few months in Socorro, New Mexico where I got hooked on radio astronomy and green chiles and haven't looked back since.

Outside of astronomy, I am the proud manager of several Madison-area recreational sports teams, including the Extragalacticos (indoor soccer) and the Infrared Sox (softball). I love to watch and play baseball (let's go, Mets!). I also enjoy reading and trying out new ice cream recipes in the offseason.
